How to Plant Peppers

Steps to Plant Peppers

  • Prepare the Soil: Choose a well-draining location or use pots with drainage holes.
  • Plant the Seeds: Sow seeds 1/4 inch deep. Space them according to variety or plant in clusters if using pots.
  • Water the Seeds: Gently water the soil to keep it moist but not soggy.
  • Provide Light: Ensure the area receives plenty of sunlight. Use grow lights if indoors.
  • Make sure you leave some space for each plant to grow.
  • Harvesting: Peppers take 60-90 days to mature. Harvest when they reach the desired size and color.

Needed Items

  • Pepper seeds
  • Seed trays or pots
  • Grow lights or a sunny windowsill
  • Watering can or hose

Watering Frequency

Water the pepper plants every 2-3 days, ensuring the soil remains consistently moist but not waterlogged.
